General Goals & SMART Goals

SMART Goals

Specific | Measurable | Achieveable | Relevant | Time-bound

  • Improve Proper Counter-Strafing,from 79% to 85%, in 2 months, Practise in FFA Deathmatch and focus on making sure you are standing completely still for every single kill
  • Improve Crosshair Placement, from 11.6 to 11, in 2 months, Practise in YPrac Prefire maps focus on memorizing all the common positions and put that same motions in real games.
  • Decrease Unused Utility On Death, from $383.7 to 300, in 1 month, Start Focusing on round timings and studying when you think the enemy is going to execute or try a play in order to effectively use your utility throughout the round in order to delay and damage them.
  • Increase Flash Blind Duration per Flash Enemy, from 2.77 sec to 2.9, in 1.5 months, learn common pop flashes for teammates and implement them during your games to have more effective flashes.

General Goals

  • Stop dying from rushes
  • Stop dying to Awpers
  • Stop accidentally over-peeking angles

Focus Areas

  • When taking fights or clearing angles do so like you are expecting an enemy there every time also, practise proper pre-aiming and crosshair placement whenever taking engagements as well as proper counter-strafing, this will win you a lot more fights.
  • Make sure you have enough ammo for your next fight, a simple way to check that is to look at your leetify stats (Spray accuracy) if it is 40% you need to have a minimum of 9 bullets with an AK or M4 before you take a fight, if it is 30% minimum of 10 bullets.
  • Trust your instincts for example, if you know you have a bad spawn don’t take the shot, if you think you got a timing go for it, by trusting your instincts and reacting off of them this will allow your gamesence to be constantly tested and while its getting tested it will get better significantly faster than if you were to play completely safe.
  • Learn between 4-7 spots on every site for the Active duty map pool, learn what kind of fights you usually take when in those spots, what weapons excel in those positions and what weapons don't, what utility can I drop, how far away can the enemy head me reload and jump from this spot, where can I fall back to.
  • learn protocols (Rules) for those positions (For example, if I get flashed, I will drop this smoke, if I just spot and see 2 enemies I will drop this molly) basic protocols, by having a minimum of 4 spots this will allow you to switch things up and if you are getting countered in 1 spot you can switch it up, as well as being unpredictable.
  • Play other maps, other than Cache and this is because getting to used to playing on 1 map will leave you fantastic on that 1 map, but then your overall skills won’t transfer to other maps like crosshair placement and preaiming, in order to evenly increase these skills throughout all maps you need to play them as well, I can see from your Matches you play a lot of cache, but hardly any, Dust2 / Nuke / Overpass / Vertigo
  • Play the vast majority of games as long matches and this is because in short matches, CS:GO is almost an entirely different game, rounds are quicker and utility doesn’t matter as much, and playing too many short games will negatively effect how you play long games, I recommend a balance of 6-1, for every 6 long games, you can play 1 short match.
